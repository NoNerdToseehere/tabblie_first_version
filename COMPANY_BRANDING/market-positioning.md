# Tabblie — Market Positioning & Go-to-Market Strategy

---

## Who We Are

Tabblie is a Dutch family company that makes eco-friendly cleaning strips — laundry, dishwasher, toilet, and all-purpose. One strip replaces one plastic bottle. Our daughter started it because she asked: "why does cleaning still need plastic?"

---

## The Problem We Solve

- **12+ plastic bottles** per household per year just for cleaning
- **80%** of cleaning products contain unnecessary chemicals
- **91%** of plastic never gets recycled
- Traditional cleaning products are heavy, wasteful, and overpackaged

---

## Our Solution

Concentrated cleaning strips that:
- Dissolve in water (hot or cold)
- Are 100% biodegradable
- Come in recyclable paper packaging
- Work as well as traditional products
- Are dermatologically tested & safe for the whole family

---

## Target Market

### Primary: Dutch Households (NL & BE)
- Young families (25-40) who care about sustainability but don't want to sacrifice convenience
- People tired of lugging heavy bottles from the supermarket
- Parents who want safe products around kids
- Environmentally aware but not "eco-warriors" — they want easy, not preachy

### Buyer Persona: "Sophie, 32, Amsterdam"
Works full-time, two kids, shops online. Wants to make better choices but doesn't have time to research everything. Attracted by simplicity ("just tear, drop, done"), design (not ugly green packaging), and the fact that it actually works.

## How We Position Ourselves

### Not This → This
- "Save the planet" → "Schoonmaken, opnieuw uitgevonden"
- Green leaves & globes → Bold orange, kraft paper, modern design
- Guilt-driven messaging → Cheeky, direct, family-friendly
- Premium/expensive eco → Affordable, accessible, for everyone
- Complicated ingredients → "Tabblie, kind kan de was doen"

### Brand Pillars
1. **Simpel** — One strip. That's it. No measuring, no heavy bottles, no mess.
2. **Eerlijk** — Transparent ingredients, honest pricing, no greenwashing.
3. **Effectief** — Works as well as the brands you know. 30-day guarantee.
4. **Familie** — Safe for kids, made by a family, for families.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch (Current)
- **DTC webshop** (Shopify) — direct to consumer, NL & BE
- **4 products:** Wasstrips, Vaatwasstrips, Toiletstrips, Allesreiniger
- **Key hooks:** Free shipping, 30-day guarantee, 15% first-order discount
- **Social proof:** Trustpilot-style ratings, UGC video testimonials
- **Foundation:** 10% of profits to environmental projects (Plastic Soup Foundation)

### Phase 2: Growth
- **Subscription model** — auto-delivery every 4/8/12 weeks (Shopify subscriptions)
- **Bundle deals** — Starter kit (all 4 products), family pack
- **Retail** — Dutch drugstores (Etos, Kruidvat), supermarkets (Albert Heijn)
- **Influencer marketing** — Dutch micro-influencers (cleaning, family, sustainability)
- **Judge.me reviews** — collect real reviews with photos

### Phase 3: Expansion
- **Belgium launch** (already shipping, increase marketing)
- **Germany** — largest EU market, strong eco-consciousness
- **Additional products** — glass cleaner strips, hand soap strips
- **B2B** — offices, hotels, cleaning companies

---

## Pricing Strategy

- **Accessible pricing** — not premium, not budget. Fair.
- **Free shipping** on all orders (NL & BE) — strips are light, shipping cost is minimal
- **Subscription discount** — 15% off recurring orders
- **First-order discount** — 15% via email signup
- **Bundle savings** — buy 3+, get discount

---

## Key Metrics to Track

- **Conversion rate** on product page (target: 3-4%)
- **Email signup rate** (target: 5% of visitors)
- **Repeat purchase rate** (target: 40% within 90 days)
- **Average order value** (target: 2+ products)
- **Trustpilot/Judge.me score** (target: 4.5+)
